About US

We are a bunch of students crazy for exploring the skies above. In a city like Delhi, where people crave for beautiful skies, Astronuts has helped in uniting astronomy enthusiasts in IIITD, providing them with a medium to practice and enjoy their passion in the field, which otherwise is neglected due to the hustle bustle of the metro life.

OUR EVENTS

Sky Observations

We oraganise night observation sessions for almost all astronomical events like meteor shower, Comet and ISS passes as well as iridium flares.

Open Telescope

To teach everyone the technical know-how of our dobsonian telescope, we conduct open telescope sessions in which students learn to set up the telescope, binoculars and other observational techniques.

Excursions

We, along with our fellow astronomy enthusiasts, anually visit the Nehru Planterium in Delhi and the Astroport in Sariska to view the beatiful skies that are amazing to witness.

Astrophysics

Introduced this year, Astronuts has embarked on a different journey, apart from our practical astronomy session we'll be covering the basics of physics involved in astronomy as well.
